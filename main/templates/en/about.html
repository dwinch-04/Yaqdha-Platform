About Us

A national cybersecurity awareness platform for members of society in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Our Vision

To be the leading reference for empowering Saudi society in cybersecurity and contributing to a safe digital environment, in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and simplified awareness content that empowers individuals to recognize digital threats and online fraud, improving overall digital security.

Who We Are

Yaqdha is a national awareness initiative and graduation project dedicated to spreading digital awareness about cyber risks and online fraud methods.

Platform Objectives

  • Raising digital awareness among users about online fraud methods
  • Educating society about cyber risks and prevention strategies
  • Helping users identify suspicious messages and links
  • Providing an interactive experience through real-world fraud simulations
  • Guiding users to official channels for reporting fraud
  • Promoting safe digital behavior when using technology and online services
