About Us

Yaqdha SA is a technical awareness initiative launched as a graduation project. It aims to bridge the cybersecurity knowledge gap among individuals within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We believe that "The human is the first line of defense." Therefore, we designed this platform to be a simplified reference that explains electronic deception methods using local examples and real-life scenarios that citizens and residents encounter daily.

Our Goals

  • Digital Awareness: Educating the community about the latest social engineering and technical fraud methods.
  • Reducing Victims: Contributing to lowering the rates of falling into fraud traps through early warning and education.
  • Security Empowerment: Providing clear guidance and practical steps to handle suspicious messages and calls.
  • Promoting Official Channels: Directing users to the correct ways to report through relevant official authorities in the Kingdom.
