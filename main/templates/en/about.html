About Us

Nuqtah SA platform is a national awareness initiative in the field of cybersecurity, dedicated to raising digital awareness among members of society in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and promoting a culture of safe use of modern technologies.

This platform comes as part of a national initiative as a graduation project aimed at spreading digital awareness among individuals about cyber risks and methods of online fraud.

Our Vision

To be the leading reference for empowering Saudi society in cybersecurity and contributing to building a safe digital environment, in alignment with the goals of Saudi Vision 2030. We believe that individuals are the first line of defense against cyber threats.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and simplified awareness content in the field of cybersecurity, aiming to empower individuals to recognize digital threats and online fraud methods, and enhance their ability to deal with them efficiently and responsibly, thereby improving overall digital security.

Website Objectives

  • Raising digital awareness among users about online fraud methods.
  • Educating society about cyber risks and ways to prevent them.
  • Helping users identify suspicious messages and links.
  • Providing an interactive experience through simulations of real-world fraud techniques.
  • Guiding users to official channels for reporting fraud.
  • Promoting safe digital behavior among individuals when using technology and online services.
